## What To Extract

- Typography scale: large hero number, medium title, small supporting text.
- Card geometry: radius, padding, elevation, and corner treatment.
- Nav style: pill-shaped tab bar with active state glow.
- Chart style: low-contrast grid, high-contrast line/area.
